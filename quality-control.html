Quality Control

Custom Packaging Quality Control Process

Five-stage QC covering prepress artwork check, color proofing, material inspection, in-process inspection and final AQL inspection with pre-shipment photo audit. Applied to every custom packaging order.

Stage 1 鈥?Prepress Artwork Check

  • Verify artwork resolution (min 300 dpi), bleed (3 mm), CMYK conversion, embedded fonts
  • Pantone color spec review and closest CMYK build for reference
  • Foil / spot UV / embossing / debossing zone alignment on the dieline
  • Barcode / GS1 DataMatrix / QR code readability test
  • Digital PDF proof sent to buyer for artwork sign-off

Stage 2 鈥?Color Proofing

  • Digital color proof (Epson calibrated)
  • Wet proof for offset jobs above 5,000 PCS on request
  • Pantone color matching on target material and coating
  • Foil sample swatch (gold, silver, rose gold, holographic) confirmation

Stage 3 鈥?Material Inspection

  • Board thickness measurement (calliper)
  • Paper weight verification (gsm)
  • Food-grade / FDA / EU 1935/2004 material certificate check for food and pharma packaging
  • Magnet strength / neodymium grade verification for magnetic boxes
  • Pouch film lamination bond strength and seal integrity test

Stage 4 鈥?In-Process Inspection

  • First-article inspection at each print run
  • Color density inspection every 500 sheets
  • Foil registration / embossing depth check
  • Die-cut precision measurement
  • Magnetic closure and structural assembly test

Stage 5 鈥?Final AQL Inspection & Pre-Shipment Audit

  • AQL 2.5 (major) / 4.0 (minor) sampling per ISO 2859-1
  • Cosmetic defect check: color, print, foil, dent, scratch
  • Structural defect check: assembly, closure, insert fit
  • Functional test: magnetic closure hold, tamper-evident seal, pouch seal integrity
  • Pre-shipment photo & video audit report sent to buyer before container loading

Certifications & Compliance

We support FSC, ISO 9001, FDA-compliant food-grade material, EU 1935/2004 food contact, REACH, GS1 DataMatrix / pharma serialization and buyer-requested third-party inspection (SGS, BV, Intertek) on RFQ.
