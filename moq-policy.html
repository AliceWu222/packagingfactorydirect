Why 500 PCS Is Our Standard Minimum

Custom packaging requires a die-cut mould, print plate setup, foil block preparation and Pantone ink mixing. Below 500 PCS the per-unit cost becomes uncompetitive and the tooling amortization outweighs the material saving. 500 PCS is the sweet spot that balances tooling amortization, buyer trial cost and factory profitability.

Small Trial Runs

For qualified brand-owner buyers, we can offer a 300 PCS trial run on selected SKUs (folding cartons, mailer boxes, stand-up pouches). Trial pricing per unit is higher than 500 PCS. Please state trial run request in your RFQ.
