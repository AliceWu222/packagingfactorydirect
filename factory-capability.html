Custom Packaging Factory Capability

In-house structural design, offset and digital printing, foil stamping, embossing, magnetic closure assembly, insert tray fabrication, food-grade materials and pharma serialization. MOQ 500 PCS, OEM/ODM only, factory direct pricing.

Production Process

  1. Structural design. Dieline drafting, 3D mockup, closure/insert engineering for magnetic boxes, mailer boxes, gift boxes, pouches, cartons and pharma packaging.
  2. Prepress. Artwork check, bleed/CMYK conversion, Pantone verification, foil positioning, barcode/QR generation, digital proofing.
  3. Printing. Offset (Heidelberg / Komori) for large volume, digital (HP Indigo) for small run, flexo for pouches/labels.
  4. Finishing. Hot foil stamping (gold/silver/rose gold/holographic), embossing, debossing, spot UV, matte / gloss / soft-touch lamination, textured paper mounting.
  5. Die-cutting & structural assembly. Magnetic closure, ribbon, insert tray (EVA/paperboard/velvet), tissue paper folding.
  6. Quality inspection. In-line inspection, AQL sampling, functional test for magnets, food-grade compliance test, pharma tamper-evident test.
  7. Packing & export. Individual polybag, master carton, palletization, worldwide shipping via sea / air / express.

OEM & ODM Support

We accept OEM (buyer supplies artwork & specification) and ODM (we design based on buyer brief). Both models are supported end-to-end from concept to delivery.
