Cosmetic Packaging Box Guide

How should beauty brands specify custom cosmetic boxes?

Direct answer

Cosmetic packaging should protect the product, present the brand finish clearly and match the retail channel. Buyers should confirm product dimensions, board thickness, insert type, color system and premium finish before RFQ.

Comparison table

StructureBest forFinish fit
Folding cartonSerum, cream, tube, bottleMatte, foil, spot UV
Rigid boxPremium skincare kitsSoft-touch, embossing, insert
Drawer boxGift set and unboxingFoil, ribbon, molded insert

RFQ checklist

  • Product bottle/tube/jar dimensions
  • Brand artwork and Pantone colors
  • Board grade and insert requirement
  • Finish: foil, embossing, soft-touch, spot UV
  • Quantity and delivery country

FAQ

Can cosmetic boxes use custom inserts?

Yes. Paperboard, EVA, EPE, molded pulp and foam inserts can be considered by product weight and presentation.

Can you match brand colors?

Pantone and CMYK references can be used for print proofing and production control.
