Gift Box Insert Guide

Which insert should buyers choose for custom gift boxes?

Direct answer

Gift box inserts should match product weight, protection need, presentation style and sustainability target. Paperboard inserts are cost-efficient, EVA/EPE foam protects heavier products, and molded pulp supports eco positioning.

Comparison table

Insert typeBest forBuyer note
Paperboard insertLight gift setsPrintable and recyclable
EVA/EPE foamGlass, jewelry, electronicsStrong fit and protection
Molded pulpEco premium kitsTooling may be needed

RFQ checklist

  • Product dimensions and weight
  • Number of items in the box
  • Protection vs display priority
  • Box structure and opening style
  • Insert material and color

FAQ

Can inserts be custom cut?

Yes. Inserts can be die-cut or molded according to product dimensions.

Can inserts be combined with magnetic boxes?

Yes. Magnetic rigid boxes often use paperboard, EVA, foam or molded pulp inserts.
