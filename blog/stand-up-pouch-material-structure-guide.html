Stand Up Pouch Material Structure Guide

How should buyers choose pouch laminate structures?

Direct answer

Choose pouch material by barrier, product weight, shelf life, filling method, display requirement and sustainability target. Common structures include PET/PE, MOPP/VMPET/PE, kraft paper laminate and PLA-based options.

Comparison table

Material structureBest forBuyer note
PET/PEGeneral dry goodsGood print and seal performance
MOPP/VMPET/PEHigher barrier snacks or coffeeBetter aroma and light protection
Kraft laminateNatural brand styleGood shelf look, confirm barrier need

RFQ checklist

  • Product type and fill weight
  • Barrier and shelf-life target
  • Zipper, spout, tear notch or hang hole
  • Printing colors and surface finish
  • Quantity and packing method

FAQ

Can stand up pouches include zipper?

Yes. Zipper, spout, tear notch and hang hole can be specified.

Can pouches be used for food?

Yes, but food-contact and destination-market requirements should be confirmed in RFQ.
