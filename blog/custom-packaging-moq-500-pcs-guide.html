When does MOQ 500 PCS make sense for custom packaging buyers?

Direct answer

Packaging Factory Direct starts custom packaging orders from MOQ 500 PCS. This is suitable for B2B buyers who need custom size, custom logo, OEM/ODM structure, branded printing and factory direct pricing without retail markups.

FAQ

Can MOQ be lower than 500 PCS?

For custom printed packaging, 500 PCS is the normal starting point. Exact feasibility depends on material, printing and finish.

Does MOQ include custom size?

Yes. Custom size and custom dieline can be supported from MOQ 500 PCS for many structures.
