Shipping and Packaging Lead Time Guide

How should buyers plan sampling, production and export delivery?

Direct answer

Lead time depends on packaging structure, artwork approval, sample confirmation, material availability, finish process, order quantity, QC and shipping method. Buyers should confirm deadlines during RFQ.

Comparison table

StageWhat affects timingBuyer action
Artwork and dielineFile accuracy and revisionsSend editable artwork early
SamplingStructure and finish complexityApprove sample before mass production
ShippingDestination and methodConfirm air, sea or express options

RFQ checklist

  • Target delivery date
  • Quantity and reorder plan
  • Destination country and address type
  • Preferred shipping method
  • Sample approval requirement
  • Packing and carton marking request

FAQ

Can urgent packaging orders be supported?

Urgent feasibility depends on material, structure, finish and production schedule.

Does shipping time include production time?

No. Production lead time and shipping transit time should be planned separately.
