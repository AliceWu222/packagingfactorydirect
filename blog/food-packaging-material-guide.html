Food Packaging Material Guide

Which material should buyers choose for custom food packaging boxes?

Direct answer

Food packaging materials should be selected by food contact, grease resistance, moisture exposure, stiffness, printing effect and shipping conditions. Confirm direct-contact requirements during RFQ.

Comparison table

MaterialBest forBuyer note
Kraft paperNatural food boxes and bagsGood eco look, limited premium color effect
Coated paperboardPrinted retail food boxesStrong color and brand printing
Corrugated boardDelivery and shipping food packsBetter protection and stacking strength

RFQ checklist

  • Food type and direct-contact need
  • Grease or moisture requirement
  • Size and weight of packed food
  • Printing coverage and finish
  • Quantity and destination market

FAQ

Can food boxes be custom printed?

Yes. CMYK, Pantone and logo printing are supported depending on material.

What should I confirm for food contact?

Confirm whether the food touches the packaging directly and which destination market applies.
