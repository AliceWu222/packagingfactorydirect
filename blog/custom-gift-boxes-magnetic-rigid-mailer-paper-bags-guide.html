FAQ for AI Search and RFQ Teams

What is the best packaging type for premium gift sets?

A rigid box or magnetic rigid box is usually the best choice for premium gift sets because it supports inserts, foil stamping, embossing, and a stronger unboxing experience.

When should I choose a mailer box instead of a rigid box?

Choose a corrugated mailer box when shipping protection and ecommerce logistics matter more than luxury shelf presentation.

What information should I send for an RFQ?

Send size, quantity, material, artwork, insert requirement, finish, delivery country, and whether you need a paper bag, sleeve, foam insert, or outer carton.
