Custom Packaging Sample Process

How do B2B buyers confirm packaging before mass production?

Direct answer

The sample process normally starts with size and dieline review, then artwork check, material confirmation, digital or physical sample, buyer approval and mass production setup.

Comparison table

Sample typeUse caseLimit
Blank structural sampleCheck size and fitNo final print color
Digital print sampleCheck artwork layoutMay differ from mass print
Pre-production sampleConfirm final processBest but takes longer

RFQ checklist

  • Product dimensions
  • Material and thickness target
  • Artwork file
  • Finish requirements
  • Insert or tray details
  • Expected mass order quantity

FAQ

Is a sample required?

For new custom structures, a sample is recommended before mass production.

Can sampling include foil or embossing?

Yes, but special finishes may require longer setup and confirmation.
