Coffee Bag with Valve Guide

What should coffee roasters specify for custom valve bags?

Direct answer

Coffee bags with valve should specify bag size, fill weight, laminate structure, zipper, degassing valve position, printing method and shelf-life requirement. The valve helps release CO2 while limiting air intake.

RFQ checklist

  • Coffee weight: 100g, 250g, 500g or 1kg
  • Valve and zipper requirement
  • Material structure and barrier need
  • Print colors and artwork
  • Quantity and destination country

FAQ

Do coffee bags need valves?

Fresh roasted coffee often uses a one-way degassing valve to release gas and protect aroma.

Can bags be custom printed?

Yes. Custom logo, full-panel artwork and matte/gloss finishes are supported.
