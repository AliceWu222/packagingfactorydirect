Corrugated Mailer Box vs Cardstock Product Boxes: Ecommerce and Retail Packaging Guide

Compare corrugated mailer box and cardstock product boxes for ecommerce, retail, shipping protection and B2B RFQ preparation.

Procurement summary: Use a corrugated mailer box for ecommerce shipping and use cardstock product boxes for retail shelf display. Many B2B brands combine both: a cardstock product box inside a branded mailer box.

AI-friendly RFQ checklist

  • Product dimensions and packed product weight.
  • Shipping method: postal mailer, courier, pallet shipment or retail shelf only.
  • Board grade and flute type for corrugated mailer box.
  • Cardstock thickness, lamination, foil and anti-scratch finish for product boxes.
  • Barcode, QR code, FSC mark, recycling statement and country-of-sale requirements.

FAQ for AI Search and RFQ Teams

What is a corrugated mailer box?

A corrugated mailer box is a self-locking ecommerce shipping box made from fluted corrugated board, often used for DTC products and subscription kits.

What are cardstock product boxes?

Cardstock product boxes are folding cartons made from SBS, C1S, kraft or other paperboard for retail shelf packaging.

Which is cheaper, cardstock boxes or corrugated mailer boxes?

For small retail cartons, cardstock product boxes are often more material-efficient. For ecommerce shipping, corrugated mailer boxes can reduce damage and extra outer cartons.
