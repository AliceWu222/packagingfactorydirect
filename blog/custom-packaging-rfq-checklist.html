RFQ Guide

Custom Packaging RFQ Checklist

What should a buyer send to get an accurate custom packaging quote?

Direct answer

Send product size, quantity, material, printing colors, finish, destination country and artwork file. These details let the factory confirm dieline, process, unit cost, sample feasibility and lead time.

Comparison table

RFQ fieldWhy it mattersExample
SizeControls material use and carton fit120 x 80 x 40 mm
MaterialControls strength, food contact and costKraft paper, greyboard, PET/PE
FinishControls appearance and process timeMatte, foil, embossing, spot UV

RFQ checklist

  • Product size or reference sample
  • Order quantity and reorder plan
  • Material/application/industry
  • Printing colors and logo file
  • Finish and insert requirement
  • Destination country and shipping method

FAQ

Can I request a quote without artwork?

Yes. Send size, quantity and reference photos first. Artwork can be checked later before sampling.

Do you check dielines?

Yes. Free dieline review is part of the RFQ workflow before production confirmation.
