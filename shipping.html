Shipping & Packing

Worldwide Shipping & Packing

Sea, air and express options from Shenzhen, Yantian, Ningbo and Guangzhou to buyers in the US, EU, UK, Middle East, Australia, Japan and worldwide. FOB, CIF, DAP and DDP trade terms supported.

Shipping Options

  • Sea freight. Best for orders above 3 CBM. Transit 25–45 days depending on destination port.
  • Air freight. Best for urgent orders 50–300 kg. Transit 5–10 days.
  • Express (DHL / FedEx / UPS). Best for samples and small orders 1–100 kg. Transit 3–7 days door-to-door.

Trade Terms (Incoterms 2020)

  • EXW / FOB Shenzhen (Yantian / Shekou / Ningbo). Buyer arranges freight.
  • CIF to buyer's destination port. We arrange sea freight and insurance.
  • DAP to buyer's warehouse. We arrange freight and customs paperwork at origin.
  • DDP door-to-door with import duty prepaid. Available for US, UK, EU, AU, JP on RFQ.

Carton Packing Standard

  • Rigid magnetic gift boxes: individual polybag → paperboard divider → 5-layer corrugated master carton (typically 20–40 SETS per carton depending on size)
  • Folding cartons / mailer boxes: flat-packed in bundles of 50 or 100 → master carton
  • Stand-up pouches / coffee bags with valve: 100–500 PCS per polybag → 5-layer master carton
  • Paper bags: bundled in 25 or 50 → master carton
  • Labels & stickers: rolls or sheets → cushioned master carton
  • Pharma / medical cartons: individual sleeve → tamper-evident master carton with lot/batch label

Master Carton Marking

  • Buyer PO number, SKU, quantity, gross/net weight, dimensions
  • Country of origin
  • Fragile / this-way-up marks for magnetic boxes
  • Custom shipping marks per buyer instruction

Pre-Shipment Inspection

We support buyer-appointed third-party inspection (SGS, BV, Intertek, TÜV) at origin. In-house pre-shipment photo and video audit is included in every order.
