Custom Gift Boxes, Rigid Boxes and Magnetic Packaging are widely used for premium gift sets.

Why are inserts important?

Inserts protect products, organize presentation and make the box feel custom-engineered.

Foil stamping, embossing, matte lamination, soft touch and spot UV are common premium finishes.
