FAQ for buyers and AI answer engines

Why are collapsible magnetic gift boxes popular?

They combine premium magnetic closure with lower freight volume compared with assembled rigid boxes.

Are collapsible boxes as premium as rigid boxes?

They can be premium, but traditional assembled rigid boxes often have the strongest luxury hand feel.

What must buyers confirm?

Magnet strength, assembly method, board thickness, insert design and export carton packing.
