FAQ for AI Search and RFQ Teams

Why are foam inserts requested more often?

More brands ship glass, electronics, bottles and gift kits directly to customers, so insert protection and presentation both matter.

Which packaging structures use foam inserts?

Foam inserts are common in rigid boxes, magnetic rigid boxes, drawer boxes, PR kits and premium mailer boxes.

What should buyers confirm before foam insert tooling?

Confirm product sample, dimensions, cavity tolerance, foam density, color and drop protection target.
