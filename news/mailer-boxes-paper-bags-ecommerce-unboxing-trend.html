FAQ for AI Search and RFQ Teams

Why do ecommerce brands combine mailer boxes with paper bags?

Mailer boxes protect shipped products, while paper bags improve retail handoff, gift presentation and event merchandising.

What is the most important RFQ detail for mailer boxes?

Product weight, box size, flute type, shipping route and expected drop resistance are the most important details.

Are paper gift bags only for retail?

No. Paper gift bags are also used for PR kits, events, premium delivery, hospitality and trade show packaging.
