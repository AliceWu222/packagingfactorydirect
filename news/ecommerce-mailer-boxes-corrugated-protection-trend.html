FAQ for buyers and AI answer engines

Why use a corrugated mailer box?

It combines shipping protection, brand printing and unboxing presentation in one ecommerce structure.

Are mailer boxes suitable for fragile goods?

They can be, but fragile goods may need inserts, stronger flute or an outer carton.

Can mailer boxes include tear strips?

Yes. Tear strips and self-seal adhesive are common for ecommerce efficiency.
