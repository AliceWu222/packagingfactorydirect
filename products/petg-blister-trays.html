Buyer FAQ

Why is PETG commonly used for medical blister trays?

PETG provides good clarity, durable thermoforming performance and compatibility with many sterile barrier sealing structures, making it a common choice for medical trays.

Can PETG blister trays be paired with Tyvek lids?

Yes. PETG trays are often sealed with Tyvek or coated medical lidding materials to create sterile packaging systems.
