Buyer FAQ

What is the benefit of a one-way degassing valve?

A degassing valve releases excess gas from freshly roasted coffee while helping prevent outside air from entering the bag, improving freshness.

Can you customize size for 250g, 500g and 1kg coffee beans?

Yes. We regularly produce custom coffee pouches for 100g to 1kg fills and can recommend suitable dimensions based on your product density.
